Generative Artificial Intelligence

This course provides an in-depth exploration of generative artificial intelligence (AI), focusing on algorithms and techniques used to create and model data that exhibits natural variability and creativity. Students will learn about different types of generative models, their applications in various domains such as image synthesis, text generation, and music composition, and the ethical considerations surrounding AI-generated content.

Learning Objectives:
->Understand the fundamentals of generative AI and its applications.
->Explore different types of generative models, including Variational Autoencoders (VAEs), Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs), and autoregressive models.
->Learn to generate realistic images, text, and music using deep learning techniques.
->Gain hands-on experience with popular deep learning frameworks (e.g., TensorFlow, PyTorch) for building and training generative models.
->Study ethical implications and societal impacts of generative AI technologies.


Topics Covered:
1.Introduction to Generative AI: Concepts and Applications
2.Probability and Statistics Basics for Generative
3.Variational Autoencoders (VAEs): Theory and Applications
4.Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs): Architecture and Training
5.Autoregressive Models (e.g., Transformers) for Sequence Generation
6.Image Generation and Style Transfer
7.Text Generation and Natural Language Processing (NLP)
8.Music Generation with AI
9.Evaluating and Improving Generative Models
10.Ethical Considerations in Generative AI

Prerequisites:
Basic knowledge of machine learning and proficiency in Python programming is required. Familiarity with deep learning concepts (e.g., neural networks) is beneficial but not mandatory.

Assessment and Grading:
Assessment will be based on coding assignments, projects where students develop and train generative models, quizzes, and a final presentation or research paper on a topic related to generative AI.

Course Materials:
*Online resources, research papers, and tutorials on generative AI
*Access to deep learning frameworks and libraries (TensorFlow, PyTorch)
*Dataset resources for training generative models
